European by design

Your data doesn't leave Europe

US-based AI services operate under US law — meaning your data can be accessed by US authorities under the CLOUD Act, regardless of where servers are located.

EuroClaw runs entirely on Hetzner, a German company, in European datacenters, using Mistral — a French AI company. 100% European supply chain.

This isn't just a privacy feature. It's the architecture.
